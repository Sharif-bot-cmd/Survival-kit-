# LEGAL NOTICE & PROJECT PROTECTION

### **Legal Basis for Protection**

This project is protected under multiple legal frameworks:

#### **International Law & Principles**
- **UN Human Rights** - Freedom of expression and scientific research
- **Academic Freedom** - Right to conduct and publish research
- **Consumer Protection** - Right to control purchased property

#### **United States Law**
- **First Sale Doctrine** - Owners' rights to modify purchased goods
- **DMCA 1201 Exemptions** - Legal protection for security research
- **Magnuson-Moss Warranty Act** - Prevents warranty voiding for modifications

#### **European Union Law**  
- **Right to Repair Regulations** - Explicit right to modify owned devices
- **Consumer Rights Directive** - Protection of consumer interests
- **GDPR Research Exemptions** - Allowances for security research

#### **Philippine Law**
- **Consumer Act of the Philippines** (RA 7394) - Consumer protection
- **Intellectual Property Code** (RA 8293) - Research and fair use provisions
- **Cybercrime Prevention Act** (RA 10175) - Requires actual damage for prosecution

## PREEMPTIVE LEGAL DEFENSES

### **Against DMCA Takedowns**

Any DMCA claim against this repository would be:

1. **Legally Baseless** - No copyright infringement occurs
2. **Anti-Research** - Attacks legitimate security research  
3. **Anti-Consumer** - Opposes right to repair principles
4. **Geopolitically Problematic** - Targets Global South developer
